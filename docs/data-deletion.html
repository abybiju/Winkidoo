Data Deletion Instructions – Winkidoo

Last updated: February 2026

How to request deletion of your data

You can request deletion of your Winkidoo account and associated data in the following ways:

  1. In the app: Open Profile (or Settings), find the account or data section, and use the option to delete your account or request data deletion if available.
  2. By email: Send an email to the support address listed in the app or on the app store listing. Include the email address associated with your Winkidoo account and state that you want your account and data deleted.

What we delete

Once we process your request, we will delete your account and the data we hold (profile, vault metadata, battle history, and any stored content such as messages or media) within a reasonable period, except where we must retain data for legal or safety reasons.

See our Privacy Policy for how we collect and use your information.
