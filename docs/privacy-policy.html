Privacy Policy – Winkidoo

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Winkidoo (“we”, “our”) is an app for couples to share surprises and interact with an AI judge. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide when signing up (e.g. email from your login provider), content you create in the app (messages, photos, voice notes), and usage data necessary to run the service (e.g. battle results, vault state). We use Supabase for authentication and data storage.

3. How we use it

We use your data to provide the app (vault, battles, judge, surprises), to improve the service, and to communicate with you about your account where needed.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your data. Content you create is shared only within your linked couple account as intended by the app.

5. Data deletion

You can request deletion of your account and data. See our Data Deletion Instructions.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at the support email provided in the app or on the app store listing.
